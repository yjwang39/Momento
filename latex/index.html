Position

  • [h] means here.
  • [t] means at the top of the page.
  • [p] means on its own page.

I usually use [ht] for small/medium figures, and [htp] for large ones. Or [H] if you want it to stay just there.

    \subfigure[pic 2]{\includegraphics[width=.7\textwidth]{2.png}}

}

Then try \lstinputlisting[caption=Source codes]{code.m} to insert source codes in LaTeX.
